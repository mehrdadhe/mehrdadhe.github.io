Mehrdad Hessar

I am a Ph.D. student working with Shyam Gollakota on low-power communication and embedded system design in the Networks and Mobile Systems Lab.
I earned my M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from EE Department at University of Washington. Before UW, I received my B.Sc in Computer Engineering at Computer Engineering department at Amirkabir University of Technology (2015).
